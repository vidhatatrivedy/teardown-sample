# Vision & Why

## One-line outcome

**Chef's Console turns chaotic corporate catering emails into confirmed, trackable bulk bookings — without losing the thread between enquiry, kitchen prep, and delivery.**

## The problem

Restaurants that serve corporate groups, tour operators, and event planners receive bulk-order requests primarily by email. Staff copy details into spreadsheets, miss follow-ups, and lose the link between the original enquiry, the confirmed booking, and the delivery order. Invoices are assembled manually. When volume grows, errors compound.

## The solution

A single workspace where:

1. Enquiries arrive from email (automated) or manual entry (AI-assisted paste)
2. Staff link each enquiry to a corporate **Client** and optional **Tour Manager**
3. One action converts an approved enquiry into a **Booking** and **Order** with shared reference IDs
4. Ops tracks payment, preparation status, and delivery on a calendar
5. Owners manage team access with role-based permissions

## Why now

Built as an MVP with AI-assisted development — it demos well and supports real workflows. The next phase requires **documented architecture and hardened multi-tenancy** before scaling customers or fundraising diligence.

## Success metrics

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Enquiry → booking conversion time | Under 5 minutes (staff-assisted) |
| Email-to-enquiry auto-match rate | 70%+ with human review on low confidence |
| Booking data entry errors | Near zero (single source of truth) |
| Time to onboard new restaurant | Under 30 minutes |

## Non-goals (see functional_scope.md)

Chef's Console is **not** a POS, inventory system, or consumer reservation app (OpenTable-style).
